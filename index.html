DATA

Where does this data come from?

The Covid data is provided via an API from RapidApi.

It doesn't specify the data source, just that it's scraping from government websites. I didn't verify this before building the tool, but I should have.

The information about entry/exit requirements is taken from US Embassy websites manually by a Data Entry team.

PLANNING A TRIP

What if I still need to travel?

It is not recommended to travel against government advice.

If you or your client insist on traveling you should check that travel insurance is not invalidated by traveling against advisories.

If you're travelling with a tour operator, you should also ensure that their liability insurance is valid under the current travel advisories.

TRAVEL ADVISORIES

What do the levels mean?


The levels are taken directly from the US State Department, and it's also worth checking the latest advice from the CDC.

  • Level 1: Exercise normal precautions
  • Level 2: Exercise increase caution
  • Level 3: Reconsider travel
  • Level 4: Do not travel

Don't forget, you'll also need proof of a negative test to re-enter the US.
